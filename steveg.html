About Steve Gunn

Steve

Steve Gunn, is an Australian born & raised Radio Presenter/Producer, Musician and Commercial Artist.

He has worked in commercial and community radio for over 25 years in Sydney Australia,

along with his Editorial cartoonist and caricature work under the pen name of "Zeg".

Born in 1967 to UK migrant parents

Steve's as proud of his English and Scottish heritage, as he is being Australian.

Steve Gunn promises to bring you an informative and entertaining music programme,

packed with interesting trivia and hopefully a few good laughs, right here on Wildstyle Radio.
