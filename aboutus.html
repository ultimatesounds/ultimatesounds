About Us

Wildstyle

At Wildstyle Radio, we’re passionate about showcasing both unsigned talent and signed artists across all genres.

We are fully licensed to broadcast music from signed artists and proudly support up-and-coming independent musicians

by giving them the platform they deserve.

Our journey began in 2016

    with Urban8Radio.

    While that project didn't achieve the success we hoped for, it laid the foundation for future growth.

    We then launched Hottunez Radio

    which also made its way into Second Life.

    Unfortunately, due to unforeseen challenges during the 2020 lockdown, that station also came to a close.

    In 2024

    we introduced Coolvibes Reloaded, which brought together an amazing group of presenters and a renewed energy.

    Although it too eventually came to an end, each chapter brought us closer to what we are today.

    Wildstyle Radio was officially launched in 2025

    and we’re proud to say it has been our most successful and professional venture yet.

    With a passionate and experienced team behind the scenes,

    we continue our commitment to broadcasting both unsigned and signed music.

    Every unsigned artist we feature is proudly added to our homepage,

    giving them the recognition they deserve.

    All of our past stations shared the same mission—to support and play music from all backgrounds.

    And now, with Wildstyle, that mission continues stronger than ever.

    Wildstyle is here to stay.
